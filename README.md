## 📖 About The Project
**ACPC Navigator** is a comprehensive web application built for students participating in the **Gujarat ACPC** (Admission Committee for Professional Courses) admission process.
Navigating engineering admissions in Gujarat can be overwhelming — hundreds of colleges, dozens of branches, multiple counselling rounds, and ever-changing cutoffs. ACPC Navigator brings all this information into **one clean, fast, and mobile-friendly platform**, so students can make informed decisions with confidence.
---
## ✨ Features
### 🎯 College Predictor
- Predict eligible colleges based on your **ACPC rank**
- **Branch-wise** filtering for precise results
- Supports both **Government** and **Self-Financed** institutes
- Category-aware predictions (OPEN, EWS, SEBC, SC, ST, TFWS)
### 📊 Cut-Off Data
- Detailed **branch-wise closing ranks**
- **Category-wise cutoffs**: OPEN, EWS, SEBC, SC, ST, TFWS
- Powerful **search and filtering**
- Mock Round 2026 & Round 3 2025 data
### 🏫 Colleges Directory
- **140+ Gujarat Engineering Colleges**
- Rich profiles: fees, district, contact info
- 🌐 Website • 📧 Email • 📞 Phone
- 📍 **Google Maps** integration
- 💺 Intake information & 💼 Placement data
### 🌿 Branch Information
- Engineering branch descriptions
- 💡 Career opportunities & future scope
- 🛠️ Skills required for each branch
### 📅 Important Dates
- Complete ACPC schedule tracker
- Registration, Mock Rounds, Merit Declaration
- Choice filling deadlines & counselling rounds
### 📋 Admission Guide
- **Step-by-step** ACPC admission process
- Counselling workflow walkthrough
- Document checklist & guidance

## 📜 Disclaimer
> ⚠️ **ACPC Navigator is an unofficial student-built platform.**
>
> All cutoff data, predictions, and information are based on **publicly available** ACPC data and previous years' results. Predictions are **indicative only** and do **NOT guarantee** admission.
>
> For official information, always refer to the **[official ACPC website](https://gujacpc.admissions.nic.in/)**.
>
> Cutoff trends may vary year-to-year based on seat availability, demand, and admission policies. Students are strongly advised to participate in **all counselling rounds** for the best chance of admission.
