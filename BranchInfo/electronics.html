Electronics & Communication Engineering

Core Electronics Branches

  • Electronics & Communication Engineering (EC)
  • Electronics Engineering
  • Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE)
  • Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering (EI)
  • Instrumentation & Control Engineering (IC)

These branches focus on circuits, communication systems, signal processing, microprocessors, embedded systems, and modern communication technologies.

Specialized Electronics Branches

  • Instrumentation & Control Engineering
  • VLSI Design
  • Embedded Systems
  • Robotics & Automation
  • Microelectronics
  • Industrial Electronics

These are derived from core electronics and focus on specific technologies.

Emerging Programs

  • Electronics & Computer Engineering
  • Communication Engineering
  • Electronics with AI Applications

These combine electronics with computing and modern technologies.

Career Opportunities

  • Electronics Design Engineer
  • Embedded Systems Engineer
  • VLSI Engineer
  • Telecommunication Engineer
  • Network Engineer
  • Instrumentation Engineer
  • IoT Engineer
  • Automation Engineer

Opportunities exist in telecom, semiconductor, electronics, defense, automation, and IT industries.

Important Note

Students interested in circuits, electronics, communication, and hardware systems should choose this branch. With programming skills, EC students can also enter IT sector.
