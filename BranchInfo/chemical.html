Chemical Engineering

Core Chemical Branch

  • Chemical Engineering

Chemical Engineering focuses on designing, operating, and optimizing industrial processes that convert raw materials into useful products using chemistry, physics, and engineering principles.

Related Chemical Branches

  • Petrochemical Engineering
  • Polymer Science
  • Food Processing Engineering
  • Environmental Engineering
  • Food Technology
  • Biotechnology
  • Biochemical Engineering

These branches focus on industries like petroleum, plastics, polymers, and food manufacturing.

Career Opportunities

  • Chemical Engineer
  • Process Engineer
  • Environment & Energy Engineer
  • Plant Manager
  • Quality Control Engineer
  • Safety Engineer
  • Petrochemical Engineer
  • Food Process Engineer
  • R&D Engineer

Opportunities exist in chemical plants, oil refineries, pharma companies, food industries, polymer industries, and government sectors.

Important Note

Students interested in chemistry, industrial processes, and problem-solving should choose this branch. Work is mostly related to large-scale industrial systems, not just lab work.

Things to Consider

  • Includes both theory and industrial applications
  • Jobs mainly in manufacturing and process industries
  • Strong basics in thermodynamics, fluid mechanics are important
  • Industries like petroleum, pharma, food need chemical engineers
