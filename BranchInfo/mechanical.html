Mechanical Engineering

Core Mechanical Branch

  • Mechanical Engineering

This branch focuses on machines, manufacturing, thermal systems, mechanics, design, production processes, and industrial operations. It is one of the most versatile engineering fields.

Specialized Mechanical Branches

  • Automobile Engineering
  • Mechatronics Engineering
  • Industrial Engineering
  • Production Engineering
  • Manufacturing Engineering

These branches focus on industries like automobiles, automation, manufacturing, and industrial management.

Emerging Programs

  • Automotive Engineering
  • Robotics and Automation
  • Smart Manufacturing
  • Industrial Automation

These combine mechanical engineering with electronics and modern technologies.

Career Opportunities

  • Design Engineer
  • Production Engineer
  • Manufacturing Engineer
  • Quality Control Engineer
  • Maintenance Engineer
  • Automobile Engineer
  • Industrial Engineer
  • Government Engineer

Opportunities exist in automobile industries, manufacturing companies, power plants, oil & gas industries, aerospace companies, and government sectors.

Important Note

Students who enjoy machines, automobiles, design, and practical problem-solving should choose this branch. It offers strong opportunities in both core industries and modern fields like robotics and automation.
