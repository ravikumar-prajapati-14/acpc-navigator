Computer & IT Related Branches

Core Computer Branches

  • Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)
  • Information Technology (IT)
  • Information & Communication Technology (ICT)

These are the most flexible and in-demand branches covering programming, software development, databases, networking, and core computer science concepts.

Specialized Computer Branches

  • Artificial Intelligence & Data Science
  • AI & Machine Learning
  • Cyber Security
  • Cloud Computing
  • Big Data Analytics
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Software Engineering
  • Quantum Computing

These branches are derived from core computer engineering and focus on specific domains.

Emerging Programs

  • Computer Science & Design
  • ICT with Computational Science

These combine computer science with other fields like design and science.

Career Opportunities

  • Software Developer
  • Business Analyst
  • Database Administrator (DBA)
  • Network Engineer
  • Cyber Security Specialist
  • QA Engineer

Computer Engineering offers the highest placement opportunities and salary packages.

Important Note

Students who enjoy working on computers for long hours and have strong interest in technology should choose this branch.
