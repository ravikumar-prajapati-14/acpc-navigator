Electrical Engineering

Core Electrical Branches

  • Electrical Engineering
  • Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE)

These branches focus on power generation, transmission, distribution, electrical machines, control systems, and industrial applications.

Specialized Electrical Branches

  • Power Electronics
  • Electric Vehicle Engineering
  • Renewable Energy Engineering
  • Energy Engineering
  • Smart Grid Technology
  • Industrial Electrical Systems

These are derived from core electrical engineering and focus on specific industries.

Emerging Programs

  • Electric Vehicle Technology
  • Renewable & Sustainable Energy
  • Energy Systems Engineering

These combine traditional electrical engineering with modern technologies.

Career Opportunities

  • Electrical Design Engineer
  • Power System Engineer
  • Substation Engineer
  • Maintenance Engineer
  • Control Systems Engineer
  • EV Engineer
  • Renewable Energy Engineer
  • Government Engineer

Opportunities exist in power plants, renewable energy, EV industry, manufacturing, railways, and government sectors.

Important Note

Students interested in mathematics, physics, and electrical systems should choose this branch. It is one of the oldest and most stable engineering fields with strong career opportunities.
