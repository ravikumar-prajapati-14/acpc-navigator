Admission Steps

Follow this step-by-step guide to complete your ACPC Engineering admission for 2026–27.

Step 01
Registration & Account Creation

Create your student account on the official ACPC portal. Fill in personal details, academic information, and upload your photograph and signature. You will receive a login ID and password via SMS and email.

Required Documents
Class 10 Marksheet Class 12 Marksheet Class 12 Hall Ticket GUJCET / JEE Scorecard GUJCET / JEE Hall Ticket Passport Size Photo Updated Aadhar Card Income Certificate Caste Certificate Non Criminal Certificate (for OBC category) Freeship Card (for SC/ST category) School Leaving Certificate
Step 02
Document Upload & Verification

Upload scanned copies of all required documents. Ensure documents are clear, legible, and in the specified format.

Formats
Photo (JPG, <200KB) Signature (JPG, <200KB) Marksheets (PDF, <200KB)
Step 03
Choice Filling

Select your preferred colleges and branches in priority order. You can update or reorder your choices during the choice filling period.

Step 04
Mock Round & Final Allotment

Mock round gives an idea before final rounds. Then 3 rounds of allotment are conducted.

Step 05
Reporting to College & Confirmation

Report to your college with all documents and complete admission process.
